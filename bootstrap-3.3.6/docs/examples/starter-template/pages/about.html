Presentation

HolidaysCam is a compagny which is going to be operational as from the beginning of the year 2017. The objective is to promote the African culture in general, and the cameroonian culture in particular.
In the past years, it has been noticed that most tourist prefare to travel to Europe, America or Asia. The African continent remains for many tourists a place to avoid. Many believe, travelling to africa is putting their lives in danger. As a matter of facts, the multiple touristic attractions offered by the African continent is still unknown by many tourists. A possible explanation to this phenomenom could be the fact that, medias always present Africa as an aggregation of misery, poverty, Hunger, horrible diseases, etc.

Cameroon is often described as "Africa in miniature", the reasoon being that, almost everything found in Africa can also be found in Cameroon. These could be the the climate, the vegetation, the rivers, the lakes, the beach,the mountains, etc.
In this order of thinking, the presentation of Cameroun will be the first Task. Secondly, the different touristic attractions of Cameroon shall be presented. Thirdly, interested Tourists will have the possibility of having answers to their questions through the FAQs submenu, and finally, the site offers a blog through which customers may leave their impressions or personal opinions.

We wish you a pleasant stay in our premisces.
